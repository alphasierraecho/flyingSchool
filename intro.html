ABOUT US

ANURAG FLYING SCHOOL is a flying school situated in the capital city of India, New Delhi. We are a registered Flying school authorised by the DIRECTORATE GENERAL OF CIVIL AVIATION(DGCA) INDIA. We have multiple instructors over here with us which are having good amount of military and civil Flying hours. Even the pilots of AIR FORCE,ARMY,NAVY and COAST GUARD prefer our flying school for completing their CPL flying hours. We have a separate tower and runway of our own which makes it super comfortable for the flyers to complete their flying hours within the reasonable amount of time. The Flying school does have highly qualified flying instructors and some ground trainig instructors as well. The school is situated in the heart of India and its obviously well connected.ANURAG FLYING SCHOOL, nestled in the heart of New Delhi, stands as a beacon for aspiring aviators, offering wings to dreams and propelling ambitions skyward. With a commitment to excellence and safety, ANURAG FLYING SCHOOL provides a comprehensive array of training programs designed to nurture and develop the next generation of pilots. Its state-of-the-art facilities and seasoned instructors ensure that students receive top-notch instruction, mastering the art and science of flight with precision and finesse. From the picturesque landscapes of initial flights to the complex maneuvers of advanced training, ANURAG FLYING SCHOOL guides its students through every stage of their aviation journey, instilling confidence and competence. With a legacy built on passion for aviation and a dedication to fostering aviation professionals, ANURAG FLYING SCHOOL continues to soar, shaping the skies and shaping futures.
